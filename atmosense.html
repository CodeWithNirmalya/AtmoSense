What makes AtmoSense different

🧮
Polynomial Regression
Custom-fitted degree-3 curve trained on 120 days of ERA5 reanalysis data to predict tomorrow's mean temperature.
🛰️
ERA5 Reanalysis
Historical data sourced from ECMWF's ERA5 archive via Open-Meteo — the gold standard in atmospheric reanalysis.
🎯
NWP Comparison
Model predictions are instantly compared against Open-Meteo's professional numerical weather prediction models.
🌡️
Comfort Index
A unique thermal comfort score ranks tomorrow's predicted temperature on a human-centric scale from cold to hot.
🔬
Advanced Mode
Scientists and developers can inspect raw data tables, model coefficients, adjustable hyperparameters, and trend charts.
🌗
Light & Dark Themes
Designed from the ground up for both modes. Off-white editorial aesthetic in light; deep atmospheric dark mode.
AtmoSense
An intelligent weather forecasting interface combining machine learning regression with professional NWP comparison. Built for clarity, depth, and beauty.
